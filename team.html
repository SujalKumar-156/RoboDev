Words from Our PI

It is my pleasure to introduce one of the most esteemed clubs of NIT Patna, the Robotics Club. Formed in the year 2015 by a bunch of not more than 8 students with vision of augmenting innovation and technical culture in the college. In this short span of time, Robotics Club managed to achieve an identity among college clubs at State-level. They are also promoting robotics among students of other nearby schools and colleges. I hope that the club continues doing so and keep making our college proud.
