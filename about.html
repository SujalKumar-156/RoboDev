About Our Club

Our History

The Robotics Club was formed in the year 2015 by a visionary group of 8 students. What started as a small initiative to augment the technical culture of NIT Patna has now grown into one of the most esteemed clubs in the institute.

Founded under the guidance of Nripendra Saroj Sir, we have spent the last decade building a legacy of innovation, winning state-level hackathons, and representing NIT Patna at prestigious events like the Smart India Hackathon.

Mission

To stimulate interest in robotics among students and provide a platform for technical growth through hands-on projects.

Vision

To create an atmosphere where students from all branches (coders, designers, hardware enthusiasts) collaborate to build the future.

Objectives

Fostering innovation, promoting automation awareness, and ensuring students from all backgrounds have access to robotics resources.

Our Facilities

Hardware Lab

Equipped with 3D Printers, Oscilloscopes, and Soldering Stations.

IoT Workstation

Dedicated space for Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and sensor integration.
