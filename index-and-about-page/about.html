About Robotics Club

Innovation • Engineering • Robotics

Who We Are

The Robotics Club of NIT Patna is a student-driven technical society dedicated to fostering innovation, hands-on engineering, and research in the field of robotics and automation.

Our Mission

To cultivate technical excellence and innovation by providing students with practical exposure to robotics, embedded systems, AI, and automation technologies.

Our Vision

To establish the Robotics Club, NIT Patna as a center of excellence in robotics education, research, and competitive engineering.

Our Objectives

Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, participate in national-level competitions, promote research culture, and prepare students for industry and higher studies.

What We Do

Competitions

Participation in robotics competitions, hackathons, and technical challenges at national and inter-college levels.

Workshops & Training

Hands-on workshops on Arduino, ROS, IoT, PCB design, drones, and software-hardware integration.

Projects & Research

Development of real-world robotics and automation projects, encouraging experimentation and applied research.

Club Milestones

2019

Robotics Club NIT Patna established with a focus on hands-on robotics learning.

2021

Participation in national-level robotics competitions and hackathons.

2023

Expansion into AI, IoT, and autonomous robotics projects.

2025

Recognition in national innovation and startup events.
