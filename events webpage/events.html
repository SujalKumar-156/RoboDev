Ongoing Projects

Smart Surveillance Rover

A Security Surveillance Rover is a smart robotic vehicle designed to monitor and protect an area. It is equipped with a camera and sensors that help in detecting movement, capturing live video, and sending alerts in case of any suspicious activity.

Obstacle Avoiding Robot

An obstacle avoiding robot is an intelligent robot that can move from one place to another without colliding with objects. It uses sensors like ultrasonic or infrared sensors to detect obstacles in its path.

Smart Dustbin

Sensor based dustbin is an automated waste management system that helps keep the surroundings clean and hygienic. It uses sensors to detect the presence of a person and automatically opens the lid when someone comes near.

Voice-Controlled Robot

A voice controlled robot is a robot that works according to spoken commands given by the user. It uses a microphone and voice recognition system to understand instructions like move forward, stop, turn left or right.
